We offer Fit-out consulting services including Building Analysis and test-fit; This service helps our client identifying their own requirements for building selection; budget; and layout.

- Test fitting Out/Test fit
Test Fit stands for the testing layout design to those buildings which possibly will become the new office location for our client (potential buildings). By doing a Test Fit, the clients can easily find out which building best satisfies their staff’s requirements.

-Building Analysis
Building Analysis is a general evaluation of the potential buildings, including basic facilities (e.g. ceiling tiles; blinds; raised floor; etc.), M/E facilities (power supply;Air conditioning system; fire fighting system; Internet system; etc.) and building’s charges (electricity; water; property management; etc.) together with the Test Fit service, we help our clients to choose the most suitable building.
